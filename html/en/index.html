Founded in 1994 by Dr. Oscar Schouten and Dr. Mario Leon Cavallaro and growing steadily ever since, with spacious self-owned offices, the Firm's philosophy puts emphasis on permanent, personalized attention to each client and the most swift, innovative, and thorough solution of all cases, at very competitive fees.

Accruing more members every year, with middle-age and young, but highly experienced and motivated associates and staff, all trained and skilled in handling the most modern software and equipment available, having ample language proficiency and profound knowledge of their area of expertise, we are a solid and dynamic law firm.

The Firm is member of the most important international intellectual property associations, including International Trademark Association (INTA), International Anti Counterfeiting Coalition (IACC), Our associates regularly attend intellectual property meetings, conferences, seminars and symposiums worldwide.
